bio.

snüff /znuf/
Garage rock trio from Padua (Italy). Andrea Davì (voice, guitar), Giorgia Malagò (voice, drums) e Alessandro Maroso (bass) play songs that are love bombs, raw and sincere, sweet and sour, with a freaky and positive attitude.
